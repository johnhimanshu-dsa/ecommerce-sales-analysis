# E-Commerce Sales Data Analysis

## Project Overview

This project analyzes an e-commerce sales dataset to understand patterns in sales performance, profitability, and discount strategies. The goal is to identify key factors affecting profit and provide data-driven insights that can help improve business decisions.

The dataset contains more than 50,000 orders across multiple product categories including Furniture, Office Supplies, and Technology.

---

## Objectives

The main objectives of this analysis are:

* Understand overall sales and profit trends
* Identify which product categories generate the most profit
* Analyze how discounts impact profitability
* Discover relationships between sales, quantity, discount, and profit
* Visualize key insights using charts and graphs

## Dataset

The dataset used for this project is the **Global Superstore dataset**, which includes information such as:

* Order Date
* Ship Date
* Customer Segment
* Sales
* Quantity
* Discount
* Profit
* Product Category and Sub-Category

---

## Key Analyses Performed

### 1. Data Exploration

Initial inspection of the dataset to understand its structure, data types, and statistical summary.

### 2. Sales Performance by Category

Analyzed total sales across product categories to identify which categories contribute the most revenue.

### 3. Profit Analysis

Calculated total profit across categories to determine which segments are the most profitable.

### 4. Discount Impact Analysis

Examined how increasing discount levels affect profitability.

### 5. Correlation Analysis

Computed correlations between numerical variables such as:

* Sales
* Quantity
* Discount
* Profit

### 6. Data Visualization

Created visualizations to clearly present insights, including:

* Profit by category bar chart
* Discount vs profit relationship
* Correlation heatmap

---

## Key Insights

Some important findings from the analysis include:

* Higher discounts tend to reduce overall profitability.
* Technology products generate the highest profits.
* Some product categories generate high sales but low profit margins.
* Discounts can increase sales volume but may negatively affect profit if too large.

---

## Business Recommendations

Based on the analysis, the following recommendations can be made:

* Limit excessive discounting to protect profit margins.
* Focus marketing efforts on high-profit product categories such as Technology.
* Review underperforming categories that generate high sales but low profits.
* Use data-driven strategies to balance discount offers and profitability.
